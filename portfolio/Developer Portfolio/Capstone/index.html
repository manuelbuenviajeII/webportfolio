About Me

Hello! I'm Manuel Buenviaje II

My career has been a journey of continuous growth, each step driven by a desire to learn and adapt. My foundation was built on the front lines as a security guard, where vigilance and integrity became second nature. Eager for a new challenge, I entered the dynamic world of BPO, sharpening my communication and problem-solving skills in a high-volume setting. This led me to the remote work landscape as a Virtual Assistant, where I became proficient in digital organization, time management, and leveraging technology to efficiently support clients. Throughout this journey, my fascination with how technology works—not just how to use it—continued to grow. This curiosity has culminated in my pursuit of web development, where I am now excited to transition from using digital tools to creating them, blending my rich background in client service with a newfound passion for coding.

My goal is to create digital experiences that are not only visually appealing but also functional, accessible, and user-friendly.
